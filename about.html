About This Site

Comer features articles, recipes and travel tips related to Spain's food scene. The word Comer means to eat (or eating) in Spanish.

This site was created as a capstone assignment in Principles of Web Design for the MCAD Interactive Design and Marketing program. All content and images are used for purely educational purposes.
